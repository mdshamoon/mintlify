### Assign access to the Service Account in Google Console[​](#assign-access-to-the-service-account-in-google-console "Direct link to Assign access to the Service Account in Google Console")

* Go to [Google Cloud Console](https://console.cloud.google.com) and sign in with your Gsuite account.

![image](/images/docs/docs/FAQ/32592458/212658353-8fed1d92-2e9d-4a9f-99c9-a359dc08698f.png)

* Click on `IAM & Admin` and select `Service Accounts`.

![image](/images/docs/docs/FAQ/user-attachments/assets/4864bf84-2ab1-44f5-957c-f80c62dc3ead) ![image](/images/docs/docs/FAQ/user-attachments/assets/bcb9735b-088a-4a8f-abce-f382e081b720)

* Access the created `Service Account` and go to `Permissions`.

![image](/images/docs/docs/FAQ/user-attachments/assets/3205a3b3-504b-4ff8-b9af-3870d8f2cc18)

* Click on Grant Access, select `New Principal`, enter the desired email address (e.g., [reporting@projecttech4dev.org](mailto:reporting@projecttech4dev.org)), and assign the role as `Owner`.

![image](/images/docs/docs/FAQ/user-attachments/assets/b6ddcaee-8e5f-4b79-94e1-19a877d9a44e)

* Click `Save`.
