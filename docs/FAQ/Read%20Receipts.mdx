## Steps to configure read receipts in the Gupshup account.[​](#steps-to-configure-read-receiptsin-the-gupshup-account "Direct link to Steps to configure read receipts in the Gupshup account.")

1 . Log in to your Gupshp account.

![image](/images/docs/docs/FAQ/32592458/220826509-62b892dd-a45f-4b7a-9473-0a9dacaef9fe.png)

2 . Click on the on your Gupshup App Name.

![image](/images/docs/docs/FAQ/glific/docs/assets/143380171/bd897771-30f5-4274-b8c3-5913d694ea3d)

3. Go to the `Webhooks` section and active all the checkboxes.

![image](/images/docs/docs/FAQ/glific/docs/assets/143380171/198e5224-4fec-4c29-ab33-9c850e3e7013) ![image](/images/docs/docs/FAQ/glific/docs/assets/143380171/0842eb9b-8583-406b-abce-e220e5351dbf)

* Once you configure Gupshup, you will be able to see the status (delivered, read, sent etc) in the column name `bsp_status` of `Messages table` in BigQuery.

![2e1b384f-5fcc-4378-b377-1407a3b0d809](/images/docs/docs/FAQ/132430123/268231468-1469fd93-2c9e-4f10-9ce7-21de4b2a3d01.jpg)

* You can add this table in your dashboards to create a Funnel Chart which will help you with more insights on the different drop-off areas/ leakages in your flow. This helps you understand your flow using data & helps you tweak it to enhance engagement.
