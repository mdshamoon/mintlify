---
title: "Find opt-in link of our NGO"
---

1. Login to your Gupshup account from [https://www.gupshup.io/developer/home](https://www.gupshup.io/developer/home)
2. Go to the WhatsApp dashboard [https://www.gupshup.io/whatsappassistant/#/whatsapp-dashboard](https://www.gupshup.io/whatsappassistant/#/whatsapp-dashboard)
3. Click on settings icon of your App.

![image](/images/docs/docs/FAQ/32592458/220825528-1f1974e7-173a-4dd7-a13d-8c7b073aa883.png)

1. Clink on the copy link under `Click URL` option to copy your opt-in link.

![image](/images/docs/docs/FAQ/32592458/220825541-f01c8946-0caa-4e00-914d-5e47eed1667c.png)
